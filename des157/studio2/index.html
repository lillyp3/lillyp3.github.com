03 Candy Hearts

This design features a display of conversation hearts, a popular candy for Valentine's Day. Instead of the usual love messages, these hearts take on a humorous approach to rejection.

This design features a heart-shaped lollipop. This lollipop is nostalgic, and relates back to elementary school and sharing valentines.
